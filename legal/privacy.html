Our Privacy Commitment

We don't collect, store, or track your personal information.
This website and its tools are designed to respect your privacy completely.

What We Don't Collect

Unlike many websites, we deliberately do not collect:

  • Personal information - No names, emails, or contact details
  • User accounts - No registration or login required
  • Tracking cookies - No behavioral tracking or advertising cookies
  • IP addresses - No location tracking or user identification
  • Tool inputs or results - Your calculations stay private on your device
  • Browsing history - No cross-site tracking

What We Do Collect (Minimal & Anonymous)

Essential Website Analytics

We use privacy-first analytics that collect only:

  • Page views - Which tools are used most (no user identification)
  • Error reports - Anonymous technical errors to improve tools
  • Performance data - Page load times to ensure accessibility
Important: This data is completely anonymous and aggregated. It cannot be used to identify individual users or track behavior across visits.

How We Protect Your Privacy

  • Netlify Analytics - EU-compliant, cookie-free analytics
  • No third-party trackers - No Google Analytics, Facebook pixels, etc.
  • Local processing - All calculations happen in your browser
  • HTTPS encryption - Secure connection for all pages

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing to:

  • Delete or modify
  • Download or export
  • Opt-out of (beyond normal browser settings)

Educational Use

This website provides educational tools for learning copyright law and open source licensing. The tools are designed for:

  • Educational and research purposes
  • General informational guidance
  • Understanding legal concepts
Legal Disclaimer: These tools provide general guidance based on US law. For specific legal advice, consult a qualified attorney.

Cookies

This website uses no tracking cookies. The only cookies may be:

  • Essential cookies - Required for basic website functionality
  • Security cookies - To protect against malicious activity

No consent banner is needed because we don't use tracking or advertising cookies.

Third-Party Services

We use minimal third-party services, all privacy-respecting:

  • Netlify - Website hosting with privacy-first analytics
  • Google Fonts - Typography (loaded without tracking)

Data Storage & Security

  • Local storage only - Any preferences stay on your device
  • Secure hosting - Industry-standard security practices

Children's Privacy

This website is safe for all ages. Since we don't collect personal information, COPPA compliance is automatic.
