Classical Piano Player

Transform historical piano roll recordings into expressive musical performances. This web application takes mechanical-sounding piano roll transcriptions from the Stanford University Piano Roll Archive (SUPRA) and applies intelligent enhancement to make them sound natural and musical.

  • 456 historical recordings from legendary pianists (1905-1925)
  • Intelligent musicalization based on performance analysis
  • Experimental Debussy analysis system
  • Authentic piano roll and modern piano visualizers
  • Professional piano samples with concert hall acoustics
Tech: JavaScript, Web Audio API, Tone.js, Canvas visualization
